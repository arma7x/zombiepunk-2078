~ZombiePunk 2078~

In 2078, zombies have taken over our world. Try your best to survive and kill as many zombies as possible. Zombies will spawn every 45 seconds & and their number continues to increase

Controls:

Call     - New Game
D-PAD    - Move
Button 6 - Lock Aim/POV
Button 9 - Fire/Shoot
EndCall  - Exit Game
